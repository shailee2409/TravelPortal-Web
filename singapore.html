Itinerary

Pick this amazing Singapore tour package and head for an exciting holiday to the spectacular island city-state bustling with energy. Explore the marvels of the glamorous city by taking the sightseeing tour, which covers all the major attractions. If you have taken the ‘with frills’ package, then some exclusive excursions and activities await you. Enjoy a spectacular Night Safari of Singapore, which begins with a fiery Thumbuakar tribal performance, followed by a guided tram safari adventure. Later, you will also watch the spellbinding Creatures of the Night show. There will also be an excursion to the idyllic Sentosa Island. Make your trip even more memorable by visiting the Singapore Flyer that offers a stunning bird’s eye view of the city.

Trip Highlights

  • Visit the Merlion statue, considered an iconic landmark of Singapore.
  • Buy trinkets and souvenirs at Chinatown, the historic market of Singapore.
  • Take a tour to a jewellery factory.
